WST Final Project: 2-Week Sprint

Game Dev Web Portal & Original Mini-Game

Project Overview

This is a fast-tracked, 14-day development cycle for 2nd Year IT Game Development majors. Pairs will develop an original mini-game and a GitHub Pages website. This project focuses on functional game loops, effective collaboration, and professional web deployment.

Goal: 1-Level Game + 2-Page Website Portal + Professional GitHub Repo.

Project Requirements

1. The Website (GitHub Pages)

  • Game Landing Page: Professional design featuring the game title, gameplay instructions, and direct play/download links.
  • Team Page: Documentation of individual roles and direct links to both student GitHub profiles.

2. The Game (Any Platform)

  • Complete Loop: Must include a Menu Screen, the core Playable Mechanic, and a final Score/Game Over screen.
  • Scope: Complexity should be simple and attainable within the 14-day sprint using assets or original art.

3. GitHub Collaboration

  • Commit History: A shared repository with at least 5 meaningful commits per student to prove collaboration.
  • Documentation: A professional README.md featuring a project description, controls, and high-quality screenshots.

Project Schedule (2-Week Sprint)

Day 1-2: Genre assignment, repository creation, and initial UI wireframing.
Day 3-7: Development of the core game loop and basic HTML/CSS site structure.
Day 8-11: Integration of the game into the web portal and UI/UX styling.
Day 12-13: Bug fixes, cross-browser testing, and README finalization.
Day 14: Final Defense and Technical Project Walkthrough.

Genre Assignment Pool

The following genres have been assigned to each group. Detailed descriptions and student assignments are below:

1. Platformer

Navigate a character across suspended platforms and moving obstacles using precise jumping mechanics. Players must avoid falling into pits or touching hazards to reach the final goal marker. Success requires balancing physics-based movement with level design difficulty.

Assigned to: Agban-Dacuba
2. Tower Defense

Protect a central base by strategically placing automated defensive units along a predefined enemy path. Each defeated enemy provides currency to purchase or upgrade your towers against increasingly difficult waves. The game ends if a specific number of enemies successfully penetrate your defenses.

Assigned to: Hau-Bisquera
3. Endless Runner

The player character moves forward automatically at a steady pace while obstacles appear at random intervals. You must use jumping, sliding, or lane-switching to avoid collisions and stay alive as the game speed increases. High scores are calculated based on the total distance traveled before the first mistake.

Assigned to: Rancap-Casul
4. Puzzle Logic

Solve specific challenges that require pattern recognition, sequence memorization, or logical deduction to progress. Unlike action games, the focus is on intellectual hurdles and "Aha!" moments triggered by clever environmental interactions. Success is determined by completing the puzzle within a limited number of moves or time.

Assigned to: Santos-Monteras
5. Top-Down Shooter

Control a character from an aerial perspective and use projectile weapons to clear waves of incoming enemies. Players must navigate through open environments while managing ammunition or cooldown timers to survive. The loop concludes once the boss is defeated or a specific kill count is reached.

Assigned to: Lao-Francisco
6. Clicker/Idle

Gameplay revolves around clicking a central object to generate a basic currency that can be spent on automated upgrades. These upgrades allow the system to generate currency even without player input, creating an exponential growth loop. The primary objective is to reach high-tier milestones through strategic resource management.

Assigned to: Estay-Ocampo
7. Turn-Based Combat

Combat is resolved through a tactical menu where players and AI take turns selecting specific actions like attacking or defending. Success depends on understanding elemental strengths, weaknesses, and the effective use of limited skill points. The game loop ends when either the player or the enemy party's health reaches zero.

Assigned to: Leynes-Barquilla
8. Rhythm Tap

Players must time their inputs perfectly with musical beats or visual scrolling cues appearing on the screen. Performance is graded based on the accuracy of the tap, ranging from "Perfect" to "Miss," affecting the final score. The challenge increases as the tempo speeds up and the patterns become more complex.

Assigned to: Lagueras-Belen
9. Stealth Escape

Navigate through a high-security level by staying out of the visible detection cones of guards and cameras. Players must use environmental cover and timing to reach the exit without triggering a loud alarm. Being spotted immediately restarts the level, emphasizing patience and careful observation over speed.

Assigned to: Banay-Serrano
10. Match-3

Players swap adjacent tiles on a grid to create a sequence of three or more identical items. Cleared tiles grant points and cause new tiles to fall into place, potentially creating massive chain reactions. The game ends when the player reaches a target score or runs out of available swaps.

Assigned to: Rodriguez-Ponday
11. Racing/Time Trial

Control a vehicle or character along a complex track with the goal of reaching the finish line as fast as possible. Players must master cornering and speed boosts while avoiding track-side obstacles that cause slowdowns. The core mechanic is the "Ghost" system or a leaderboard that tracks the best completion times.

Assigned to: Lagatuz-Duran
12. Visual Novel

Experience a narrative-driven story where progress is made by reading dialogue and viewing character illustrations. At critical moments, the player is presented with choices that branch the plot in different directions. The game concludes when the player reaches one of the several scripted endings based on their decisions.

Assigned to: Matre-Rosos
13. Bullet Hell

A high-intensity shooter where the screen is filled with beautiful but deadly patterns of enemy projectiles. Players have a very small "hitbox" and must navigate through microscopic gaps in the fire while counter-attacking. Survival depends on extreme focus and high-precision movement controls.

Assigned to: Iloreta-Uy
14. Tycoon/Manager

Act as an administrator of a system, such as a shop or a theme park, by balancing budgets and customer satisfaction. You must invest earned profits into better facilities to attract more revenue and expand your operations. The win condition is usually reaching a specific net worth or prestige level.

Assigned to: Ancheta-Tan
15. Side-Scroll Brawler

Move across a 2D plane and defeat groups of enemies using a combination of melee strikes and special moves. Players are often blocked from progressing until all enemies on the current screen have been cleared. This genre emphasizes "combat feel" and managing multiple attackers simultaneously.

Assigned to: _________________________
16. Card Battler

Conflict is resolved by playing cards from a deck that represent different creatures, spells, or defensive maneuvers. Players must manage a limited resource (like mana) to play cards strategically against an opponent’s hand. The match is won when the opponent’s life points are fully depleted.

Assigned to: _________________________
17. Memory Match

Flip over two face-down tiles at a time to find identical pairs hidden within a grid. Players must remember the positions of previously revealed tiles to clear the entire board efficiently. The game tracks the number of attempts or time taken, rewarding those with high concentration.

Assigned to: _________________________
18. Physics Sandbox

Use a variety of tools and objects that interact according to the laws of gravity, friction, and momentum. Players are given a goal, like knocking down a structure or getting an object to a specific spot, using creative engineering. There is often no single "correct" solution, encouraging experimentation with the physics engine.

Assigned to: _________________________
19. Maze Crawler

Navigate through a complex labyrinth where the primary challenge is finding the correct path to the exit. Players may encounter locked doors that require keys or one-way passages that prevent backtracking. The game emphasizes spatial awareness and exploration within a restricted environment.

Assigned to: _________________________
20. Survival Defense

The player controls a single unit that must withstand constant attacks from waves of enemies for a specific duration. You must collect experience or power-ups dropped by enemies to strengthen your character in real-time. Survival is the only win condition, requiring constant movement and optimal ability selection.
